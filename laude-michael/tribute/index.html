Kobe Bryant


1978-2020
Basketball Player
Kobe Bryant was an American professional basketball player. He spent his 20-year NBA career with the Los Angeles Lakers and regarded as one of the greatest players of all time.

Career Achievements

5-time NBA Champion




2008 NBA MVP




2-time NBA Finals MVP




18-time NBA All-Star




9-time NBA All Defensive First Team




2-time NBA Scoring Champion

Life Story

Early Life


Kobe was born in Philadelphia in 1978 and raised in Italy for the majority of his childhood. He was recognized as a the top high-school basketball player at Lower Merion High School Not long after his graduation from high school, he declared for the NBA draft.





NBA Career


In 1996, Kobe Bryant got selected by the Charlotte Hornets with the 13th overall pick and got traded to the Los Angeles Lakers. From 2000-2002, he led his team to win three Finals wins in a row, which was only done four times ever before they did. To cement his legacy as an all-time great, in 2009-2010 Kobe won two more NBA Championships.





Post-NBA


After his retirement from the NBA, he ventured into storytelling. Not so long after his retirment, he produced a short film called "Dear Basketball" which then won an Oscar. He then became business focused, an invvestor of Dell, Alibaba, Epic Games, Body Armor and many more. Unfortunately, he died at the age of 41 with his 13-year-old daughter Gianna, in a helicopter crash.
