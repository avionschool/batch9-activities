Rapsody

Granger fills his gun with all bullets and shoots them forward with each bullet dealing 45 / 75 / 105 / 135 / 165 / 195 (+70% Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage to enemies for a maximum damage of 270 / 450 / 630 / 810 / 990 / 1170 (+420% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage.

Rondo

Granger dashes in the designated direction and his next 2 Basic Attack.png Basic Attack will deal extra 10% / 14% / 18% / 22% / 26% / 30% damage in the next 5 seconds. Each time Rhapsody.png Rhapsody's bullets hit an enemy unit, the cooldown of this skill will be reduced by 0.5 second. This skill can be blocked by Grounded effect.

Death Sonata

Granger transform his violin into a Super Cannon and fills it all with Super Bullets. During this period, he is unable to cast any other skills or perform Basic Attack.png Basic Attack. Granger can shoot up to 3 Super Bullets, and he can roll to another location after shooting out a Super Bullet. The Super Bullets ignores enemy minions & creeps and only hits enemy heroes. The Super Bullet deals 80 / 120 / 160 (+70% Total Physical Attack) + 12% of the target's lost HP points of Physical Damage to enemies hit and slows them by 80%. When this skill's duration ends, for every 2 remaining Super Bullets left, he will reduce the cooldown of this skill by 15%. Granger can shorten the time needed to load the next Super Bullet if his roll is interrupted by an obstacle. This skill can be cancelled/interrupted by Crowd Control effects in during Super Bullets.

Caprice

Granger fills his gun with 6 bullets at a time. Every 6th bullet deals Critical Icon.png guaranteed critical damage. Granger's Basic Attack.png Basic Attack will deal more damage, but he only gains 50% Attack Speed from Equipment or Emblems.
