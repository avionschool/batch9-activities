IU

Full Name: LEE JI EUN
Date of Birth: May 16, 1993


IU (아이유) is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress currently under EDAM Entertainment. She debuted on September 18, 2008 with a stage on M Countdown for the song "Lost Child".

Her stage name is derived from the phrase "I and You", symbolizing that people can become one through music.

IU decided to become a singer when she was 14 after singing at her school's sports competition, as she felt happy receiving the audiences' applause. She started to attend auditions, but failed all 20, and she was also scammed by fake entertainment companies. When IU was in her seventh grade, her family started to have financial problems due to the failure of her father's project. IU had to live apart from her parents in a studio room with her grandmother, younger brother and cousin for a year and a half, in conditions of great poverty.

IU debuted on Mnet M! Countdown on September 18, 2008 with her debut single "Lost Child". It was later released as the title track for her debut mini album Lost and Found on September 24, 2008. However, she did not gain mainstream popularity until her follow-up and debut studio album Growing Up.

On April 16, 2009, IU launched her first full album, titled Growing Up, with the lead promotional number "Boo". Within three weeks, "Boo" went up to the No.1 contenders for SBS Inkigayo, and her name also went up twice on Take 7 together with other notable artists like SG Wannabe and Younha. The song also did well on KBS Music Bank K-Chart, on Mnet M! Countdown chart, and on various online music charts.

Fun Facts and Trivia

IU is one of the most influential female celebrities in South Korea. She is one of the highest earners and has received numerous awards for her acting and music.

IU was a trainee for Good Entertainment with Uee, Yubin from Wonder Girls, Heo Ga-yoon from 4Minute, and Jun Hyoseong from Secret.

IU’s family struggled financially. She and her brother lived with her grandmother and cousins in conditions of poverty and apart from her parents for over a year during her middle school.

IU failed an audition at JYP but got into Leon Entertainment. Later JYP said that he was going to fire whoever let IU go

Age trivia: IU was 15 years old (15 international age; 16 Korean age) when she debuted as a soloist in 2008.

Height trivia: IU’s height is 162 cm (5'4''). Other kpop idols with the same height are: Jisoo (Blackpink), Jihyo (Twice), and Dara (2NE1).

Ideal type: IU used to say her ideal type was Taeyang (Big Bang). Some famous kpop idols that have listed IU as their ideal type or are known to be her biggest fanboys are: Jungkook (BTS), Yoseob (Highlight), actor Kim Soo Hyun, Suho and Kai (EXO).

Friendship: IU is very good friends with Suzy (Miss A), Jiyeon (T-ara), actress Yoo In Na, Sulli (fx), and Goo Hara (Kara)

Career Timeline

2008

IU made her solo debut with the single Lost Child. Her first album was not successful.

2009

IU launched her first studio album and promoted the new single Boo, a huge style shift from Lost Child. IU started gaining more favorable attention.

2010

IU released the song Nagging with 2AM’s Lim Seul Ong. The song was one of the theme songs for the show We Got Married season 2.

2011

IU was part of the main cast of the variety show Heroes. She also made her acting debut in the drama Dream High.

2012

IU was recognized by Forbes Korea as one of the most influential people in South Korea.

2013

IU was the lead actress in the drama You Are the Best!

2014

IU won the 2014 KBS Drama Best New Actress award.

2016

IU acted as the female lead in Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo.

2019

IU was the female lead in the drama Hotel del Luna.
