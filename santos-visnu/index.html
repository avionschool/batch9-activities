Founder-acharya of ISKCON

  • HOW HE STARTED THE MOVEMENT

    In 1922, he eets for the first time Srila Bhaktisiddhanta Sarasvati Thakura – his future initiating spiritual master – who asks him to preach Lord Chaitanya’s mission in the Western countries. This is where the idea of International Society for Krishna Consciousness or ISKCON began. He traveled to USA to start a movement teaching God consciousness.

  • FOR THE FIRST TIME, WEST LOVED EAST.

    He sat at the center of New York Central Park and started to sing a very simple tune of Hare Krishna Maha Mantra. Surrounded by people whose beliefs and ways of life are very different, a monk who sings an unknown language and wears different clothes caught the attention and interest of the curious generation.

    His teachings, to always remember God and never forget him, spread fast accross the city. And that time, he also caught the attention and interest of the most famous band, The Beatles. They invited the Swami, who suddenly replaced their names in headlines, to the radio station out of coriousity. That event made ISKCON and Srila Prabhupada more famous across USA. That aso marks the start of spiritual journey of their band member, George Harisson, who soon decided to take a more simple and meaningful path in life.

  • MISSION ACCOMPLISHED

    The main mission of the movement is to spread God awareness to each and everyone. It should not matter whether you're a Christian, Buddhist, Muslim, etc., we should always remember God and never forget him. Through this, we could all live in a loving and harmonious world.

    At this present time, there are more thousands of ISKCON temples and centers all around the world. More and more people are practicing vegetarianism and veganism, more and more people are taking the path of a much meaningful life, and the most important thing, more and more people are becoming aware of God.

  • SERVING GOD UNTIL HIS LAST BREATH

    After he gave up all of his material posessions and devoted his life to serve God, his material body whose bound to laws of material nature gave up in November 14, 1977. In his last days, despite of having dificulties in breathing and being told by his desciples to take a rest, he continued translating books and instructed all of his loving desciples to think about God instead of giving their attention to his condition. They all sang the Holy Names of the lord until his very last breath.

    Until now, all of his desciples are still following his instructions. ISKCON devotees never stopped spreading the Holy Names of the lord through chanting, books destribution, Food for Life, and rendering service to attain Prema-Bhakti or Love of God.
